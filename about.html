About us

The Founder of we Pro began his security career in 2008. Since then, he has led numerous security projects, presented at international conferences, and published scientific papers in both academic and industry fields. He is also the co-founder of MrLinuxer Company launched in 2014. His professional journey includes serving as a security engineer in several IT companies and private th data centers. On 11 June 2025, he founded wePro Company Limited, a turnkey engineering services provider and system integrator based in Myanmar. wePro delivers high-quality solutions to international organizations and private enterprises. Our vision is to build a truly global company—one that achieves sustainable growth, values its employees, and contributes to customers and society through responsibility, teamwork, honesty, and a strong family spirit. With extensive expertise in engineering, we provide complete turnkey solutions that open new opportunities for our clients' businesses. Our reputation is built on technical excellence, proven experience, internationally recognized certifications, and a strong commitment to exceptional customer service. Today, we are expanding beyond Myanmar by building strategic partnerships with leading international companies in Security & Safety, IT Infrastructure, Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Software Development, Telecommunications, and Equipment Manufacturing & Supplies.
